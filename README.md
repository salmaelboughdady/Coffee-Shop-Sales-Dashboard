This project features an interactive sales dashboard for a coffee shop that helps in identifying peak service hours, high-performing product categories, and revenue growth patterns during the first half of 2023 (Jan-June).

## Data Transformation
To enable deep-dive analysis I engineered **six calculated columns** within the data that allowed for more effective and clearer analysis:
- **Revenue**: To aggregate total sales value per transaction.
- **Month and Month Name**: To facilitate chronological sorting and user-friendly trend labeling.
- **Day of Week & Day of Week Name**: To analyze weekday vs. weekend performance.
- **Hour of Transaction**: To identify peak customer footfall throughout the day.

## Dashboard Features
- A line chart tracking Total Revenue from **January** to **June** (2023).
- A breakdown of transactions by hour to identify **rush hour** patterns.
- A breakdown of transactions by **day of week**.
- A bar chart tracking No of Transactions by **Product Category**
- Tabular analysis **(Pivot Table)** of the **Top 15 products by No of Transactions** to identify most popular products.
- Interactive Excel Slicer for store locations.

## Key Analytical Insights
- Transaction volume peaks significantly during the morning rush between **8:00 AM and 10:00 AM**.
- The business demonstrated consistent month-over-month growth, reaching a peak of **$166,486**.
- **Coffee** achieved the greatest no of transactions followed by Tea and Bakery.
- The busiest day of the week is **Friday** (21,701 transactions), while **Saturday** is the slowest day (20,510 transactions), though it only represents a minor **5.5% decrease** from the Friday peak.
